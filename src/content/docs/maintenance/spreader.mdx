<Aside type="caution" title="Before you start">
Use appropriate footwear, eye protection, and clothing when performing maintenance work on the spreader.
</Aside>

## Regular Maintenance and Checks

* Use a hose or wipe excess material off of the spreader.
* Open and close the gate and verify that the gate closes and opens fully.
* Check for presense of the agitator wire.
